About Us

We at FaceTunes combine facial expression analysis with music to provide a personalized music experience. Our team of experts has developed cutting-edge algorithms that analyze your facial expressions, enabling us to accurately identify your emotions. With this information, we curate a tailored playlist that aligns with your mood.

We are a team of passionate individuals, consisting of experts in artificial intelligence, facial recognition technology, and music curation. Together, we have built a cutting-edge system that goes beyond simply analyzing facial expressions. We have fine-tuned our algorithms to capture the subtleties of emotions, allowing us to provide you with accurate and meaningful recommendations.
